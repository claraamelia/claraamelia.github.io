About Clara:

I was born in March 9, 2000, in San Juan, Puerto Rico to Cuban parents. Ever since I was a little kid, I've been connected to letters and art. I would write and illustrate my own short stories at age 9. Ten years later, I'm writing about fashion, arts and culture, film, and issues surrounding Puerto Rico. I am also drawing but as a hobby.

I am a sophomore at The New School, studying Journalism and Culture and Media, hoping one day Teen Vogue or Vulture hire me. In high school, I broke the record for the most views on an article in their news website, which prompted my teacher and classmates to give me a round of applause as I sank into my seat.

Before the 2018 distasteful Academy Awards, I published an article for Miuse Magazine that was deleted after some rebranding but you can find it in my Writing page.
